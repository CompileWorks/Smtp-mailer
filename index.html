How to Get Your EmailJS Details

  1. Sign in to your EmailJS dashboard
  2. Your Public Key is already configured (see above)
  3. Your Service ID is in the Email Services section (you need to set up an email service first)
  4. Your Template ID is in the Email Templates section (you need to create a template first)
  5. Save your configuration to enable email sending

How It Works

  1. Configure your EmailJS settings with your Service ID and Template ID
  2. Upload HTML email templates using the Upload tab
  3. Select a template and enter recipient details in the Send tab
  4. Send your HTML emails directly through EmailJS
  5. All templates are stored locally in your browser
