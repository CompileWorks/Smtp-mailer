How to Use This SMTP Mailer

  1. Enter your SMTP server details (host, port, username, password)
  2. Click "Save Configuration" to store your settings
  3. Choose between sending a single email or bulk emails
  4. For bulk emails, prepare a CSV file with recipient data
  5. Use placeholders like {{name}} and {{email}} in your templates
  6. Click send and wait for the emails to be delivered

Note: For Gmail, you may need to generate an App Password if you have 2FA enabled.
